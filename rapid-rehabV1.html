Who We Are

Arul Kandan

Senior Consultant Physiotherapist
BPT · MSc · MCSP · HCPC (UK)

At Rapid Rehab At Home, we deliver premium home-based rehabilitation through a dedicated team of healthcare professionals led by Senior Consultant Physiotherapist Arul Kandan. With over 25 years of international clinical and leadership experience, Arul has led therapy departments and developed rehabilitation services across the United Kingdom, Ireland, Malaysia and India.

We were founded with a clear purpose: to make specialised, hospital-grade rehabilitation accessible in the comfort and safety of patients' homes. Through advanced, evidence-based programmes we bridge the gap between acute hospital care and long-term independence — turning familiar surroundings into the most effective environment for recovery.

Our Vision

To redefine the standard of home-based rehabilitation — building a community where ageing, neurological conditions, injury or surgery never determine a person's independence or quality of life.

Our Mission

To deliver highly specialised, compassionate and effective physiotherapy directly to our patients' doorsteps — accelerating recovery, restoring confidence and maximising physical independence.

Why Home-Based Rehab

The best place to relearn how to live your life is where you actually live it.

Clinics teach you to walk on flat polished floors. We teach you to walk on the rug between your sofa and kitchen — the journey that matters most.

Real-World Progress

Practice climbing your stairs, navigating your kitchen, getting in and out of your bed — the goals that actually matter.

Zero Travel Stress

No painful car journeys, no waiting rooms. Every session begins the moment your therapist arrives.

Family-Centred Care

Your loved ones are coached alongside you — building a confident, capable support team at home.
