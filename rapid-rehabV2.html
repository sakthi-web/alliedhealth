Why Home-Based Rehab

The best place to relearn how to live your life is where you actually live it.

Clinics teach you to walk on flat polished floors. We teach you to walk on the rug between your sofa and kitchen — the journey that matters most.

Real-World Progress

Practice climbing your stairs, navigating your kitchen, getting in and out of your bed — goals that actually matter.

Zero Travel Stress

No painful car journeys, no waiting rooms. Every session begins the moment your therapist arrives.

Family-Centred Care

Your loved ones are coached alongside you — building a confident, capable support team at home.

Our Specialist Services

Every programme begins with a face-to-face home assessment and ends when you've reclaimed what matters most — expert assessment and treatment across neurological, orthopaedic and musculoskeletal conditions, tailored to your goals.

01 · Neurological

Neurological Rehab

Neuro-plasticity focused exercises, balance training, gait retraining and upper-limb rehabilitation to rebuild real independence.

Includes
Stroke Parkinson's Multiple Sclerosis Brain Injury Spinal Cord Injury
02 · Orthopaedic

Orthopaedic Rehab

Targeted strengthening, scar-tissue management, joint mobilisation and safe gait training to get you moving pain-free.

Includes
Hip Replacement Knee Replacement Fractures Spinal Surgery
03 · Elderly Care

Elderly Care & Mobility

Confidence-building mobility drills, home safety assessments and strength training to maintain freedom & dignity.

Includes
Fall Prevention Frailty Deconditioning Chronic Joints
04 · Spine & Nerve

Spine & Nerve Care

Comprehensive assessment and targeted rehabilitation for spinal and nerve-related conditions affecting movement and quality of life.

Includes
Sciatica Whiplash Chronic Neck/Back Pain Spinal Dysfunction Disc Issues
05 · Post-Op

Orthopaedic & Post-Op Rehab

Expert rehabilitation following joint surgery and orthopaedic procedures, safely restoring strength, range of motion and confidence.

Includes
Hip & Knee Arthritis Post-Op Replacements Fractures Patellofemoral Pain
06 · Limb Injuries

Upper & Lower Limb Injuries

Targeted treatment for soft-tissue and joint injuries of the arms, shoulders, legs, ankles and feet — from acute to chronic.

Includes
Tennis Elbow Carpal Tunnel Achilles Plantar Fasciitis Rotator Cuff

At Rapid Rehab At Home, we deliver premium home-based rehabilitation led by Senior Consultant Physiotherapist Arul Kandan. With over 25 years of international clinical experience, Arul has led therapy departments across the UK, Ireland, Malaysia and India.

Founded with a clear purpose: to make specialised, hospital-grade rehabilitation accessible at home — bridging the gap between acute hospital care and long-term independence, turning familiar surroundings into the most effective environment for recovery.
